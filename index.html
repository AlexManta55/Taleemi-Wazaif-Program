Benazir Taleemi Wazaif Program

The Benazir Taleemi Wazaif Program is a conditional cash transfer program in Pakistan designed to provide high school education to the children of active dependent beneficiaries of the Benazir Income Support Program. Through additional cash support, beneficiaries can enroll their children in schools or colleges and increase the sum assured by reducing education fees.

Main Objectives

  • Endorsing the importance of primary, secondary, and higher secondary education among families deserving of the Benazir Income Support Program.
  • Enhancing enrollment in children's schools.
  • Improving children's school attendance.
  • Reducing the enrollment rate of children in school.

Steps to Participate

  1. Verification of Eligibility: First, you must confirm that you belong to an eligible family under the Benazir Income Support Program. You have to apply for this by visiting your local Benazir Income Support Program office or website.
  2. Preparation of Documents: You have to prepare your authentic identity card number, the name of the eligibility verification agency, and other necessary documents.
  3. Applying: Apply by visiting the Benazir Income Support Program office or website with your authentic documents.
  4. Verification of Eligibility: Your application will be reviewed to confirm eligibility.
  5. Registration: Once your eligibility is verified, you will be allowed to register for the Benazir Education Scholarship Program.

For more information, you can visit the Benazir Income Support Program website or the Benazir Taleemi Wazaif Program.
